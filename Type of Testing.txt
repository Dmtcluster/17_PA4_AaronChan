Unit Testing
What is unit Testing?
Unit testing is the execution of a complete class, routine or small program

Unit Testing is conducted by single programmer or team of programmers
It is type of whitebox testing

Integration Testing
What is Integration Testing?
Integration testing is the combined execution of two or more classes, packages, components, or subsystems

Integration Testing is conducted by multiple programmers or programming teams
It is type of blackbox & whitebox testing
